## Combat Tips

- **Normal Attack**: No mana cost, reliable damage
- **Abilities**: High mana cost, powerful effects
- **Items**: Use potions when HP/mana is low
- **Defend**: Reduces incoming damage by 50%
- **Run**: 50% chance to escape (no rewards)

## Survival Tips

1. Always keep 2-3 health potions
2. Don't fight if HP is below 30%
3. Return to Village to buy supplies
4. Save your game regularly (option S)
5. Use abilities on tough enemies
6. Explore one area at a time
7. Complete quests for big rewards

## Controls
